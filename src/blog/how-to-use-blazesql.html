How to

How do I use BlazeSQL AI?

BlazeSQL is an AI chatbot that can answer your questions by giving you the relevant data, an SQL Query, or even a graph. By looking at the names of the tables and columns in your database, it can write the correct SQL query that will get the data that answers your question (Ex. "How many customers from italy did we get last week?"). If you connect your database, the BlazeSQL desktop app can then automatically run the resulting query and show you the data or even graph the result, without the data ever leaving your computer (keeping your data secure).

You start by adding your Database Info to BlazeSQL, either by copy/pasting the table and column names from your database, or by directly connecting your database using the credentials. After that, you are taken to the chat window where you can directly ask the chatbot any question you might have, Ex. "How many products did we sell each month this year, grouped by country?". The chatbot will respond with a query, Which you can copy, or run (if your database is connected), and even graph or add to a BlazeSQL dashboard.

What if the AI doesn't know everything it needs to know about my database?

While the AI has deep knowledge of many industries (it even passed Law and Medical exams), it's possible that there are details specific to your database, or your company, that only people who work at your company know. Any important information like this can be added in the database page. The database page allows you to add descriptions and other notes to each table, or even individual columns. The AI will see this information every time you talk to it, so it has everything it needs to give you what you want.

Once you've set up your database, you get the following:

  • SQL queries generated for you. All you have to do is ask a question, or say what data or query you want - The AI will immediately use its expert knowledge and your database info to write you a query..
  • Running queries & correcting errors. If you connect your Database with the desktop version, you can automatically run queries generated by the AI, and the AI will automatically fix any errors.
  • Graphs & Dashboards. Want to visualize a result? Simply click Options, and then click Graph. The AI will automatically pick the best graph to visualize your query, and you can add it to a dashboard with a single click..

By using BlazeSQL as an assistant that does most of this work for you, you can speed up your workflow and boost your productivity. If you ever get stuck, or have any questions at all, BlazeSQL customer support can help you very quickly. All you have to do is click the customer support chat bubble at the bottom left for a live chat with our team, or email support@blazesql.com
