Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe with BlazeSQL?

Yes, data rows in your database will never be uploaded to our servers or anywhere else. To generate queries, the AI only needs to see the names of your tables, and the names of the columns. If you connect your database with the desktop version, you can automatically run the queries, and the results will go directly from your database to your computer and stay 100% local and private. Graphs and dashboards will use this local data.

Do I need to upload my data?

No, all database rows remain private, the AI only needs to see the table names and column names to be able to write the queries.

Can the AI create complex queries?

Yes, there is no limit to the complexity of queries. For more complex queries, you may need to be more specific with your request, or provide clarification.

How can the AI understand things that are specific to my database/company?

The database page allows you to add important information about tables and columns. This allows the AI to understand and remember important details about your database and your company.

Can queries be incorrect?

Without enough information, queries can occasionally be incorrect. If the AI gets stuck, or non-technical users are suspicious of the answers, they can simply flag the conversations for technical users to check out and fix. This will also help the AI learn and improve for next time.
